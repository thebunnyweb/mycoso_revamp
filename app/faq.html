F.A.Q

Hi and welcome to Mycoso! We hope these FAQs clarify some of the doubts you may have. If you have any doubts that aren't addressed here, feel free to reach out at hello@mycoso.com

  • How does Mycoso work?

    We are a content solutions agency. That means we cover everything related to content marketing for our clients. It depends on what the client needs - a full blown content solutions package, a content marketing plan, or blog posts for their website. Check out our Services Page. As of now, we only need freelancers to write for our clients, but the rate at which we're growing, you may be able to do more, if you're so inclined! We'll keep you updated about that :)

  • How is the price set?

    The price depends on the depth of research required and will be negotiated by Mycoso with the client, irrespective of whether they are standalone articles or part of a package. We value our expertise and yours as well. Plus, when we have a job available, you'll see how much money you'd be able to make if you choose to do it. So essentially, you pick the price you're willing to write for!

  • Why should I work through Mycoso?

    We've been freelancers. We know pitching is hard. You needn't do that here. We are the ones responsible for bringing on clients. All you have to do is write! Makes it easier for you, right? And as a freelancer, we know that it's good to have a choice of platforms and clients available. Mo' choices, mo' money!

  • Is my payment guaranteed in a fixed amount of time? Freelancers often face lax timings in terms of payments.

    Oh, we know that issue very well. Mycoso charges 50% to clients upfront as soon as the project starts. We also ensure that the final service we provide is only released once the payment comes in. We will see your progress, though. So you can't slack off! Rest assured, Mycoso will provide the final delivery to the client only once the full payment is made. Once they are satisfied,and corrections (if any) are done, the payment will be released to you!

  • Will I get all the projects I apply for?

    If you're the best person for the project, absolutely! What's great is that there will be a variety of industries and sectors, so you can pick and choose what you do best. We also absolutely encourage branching out. If you really would like to work in a project outside your area of expertise, reach out and we'll see what we can do!

  • Why do you have a test before I can start applying for work?

    It's a pretty basic thing and it's just for us to ensure you're able to do a pretty good job. Efficiency is our God, and we would rather have awesome writers like you associated with Mycoso. We don't want to turn into a free-for-all platform that cannot provide assurance of quality. Wouldn't you prefer that as well? Don't worry, they won't be weird questions about what a gerund or a dangling participle is. I'm sure some of us here don't know what those are either.

  • Can I write in other languages apart from English?

    We aren't thinking about that as of now. However, talk to us at hello@mycoso.com and let us know what you're thinking. We'd love to have you on the list for (if and) when that does happen!

For Clients

  • How am I assured quality?

    We have worked in the content, digital, and writing space for a while now. We know from experience what works and what doesn't. Things change every day in the digital world, and a big part of our job is to stay updated. We work with you through your pain points so that we are on the same page before we start work. You'll find more information on the Services Page.

  • Do I get to pick the writer I want?

    We have our internal criteria for selecting writers for relevant projects. Rest assured we will pick what's best for your company and your goals

  • Why should I work with Mycoso?

    We are absolutely committed to making the internet a little better with smart strategy and people-oriented content. There are enough random posts out there that are just focused on clickbait and keyword stuffing. We don't do that and never will. We think long term and want to position your company as an institution to look up to. We make a real difference, and we're always honest about what we think is best for your company and its future. If you're looking for honest partners who want to see your company grow and will be there to tell you what's working and what isn't, you're in the right place.

  • What are the payment options?

    As of now we do bank transfer, Paypal and PayTM. We are in the process of setting up encryption on the site to ensure that money transfers go through the website easily. As safety is paramount, we'd rather stick to our banking institutions as of now, till we have everything perfectly in place.

  • Do you provide add on services?

    Absolutely, check out our Services Page for the different types of services we offer. If there's something more that you'd like to discuss, reach out to us at team@mycoso.com and we'd be happy to create something for you!

  • Do you provide content in other languages apart from English?

    We aren't thinking about that as of now. However, talk to us at hello@mycoso.com and let us know what you're thinking. We can try to figure something out, and if we think we can do justice, sure. However, we are likely to refuse if we feel we cannot do a good job. We are focused on providing quality to our clients, after all.
